Pricing & Pickup FAQs

Scrap metal prices are tied to global commodity markets which fluctuate based on supply, demand, and economic factors. Our rates reflect these daily changes to ensure fair pricing for both our customers and our business.

Our team weighs your materials on calibrated scales during pickup. The price is calculated based on the current rate per kg for each material type. Clean, sorted materials typically command higher prices than mixed scrap.
